Insights

  • THE EVOLUTION IS MORE CUALITATIVE THAN CUANTITATIVE.

    Use is focused on globalizing networks. These networks
    have shifted from socializing networks to networks with
    practical functions.

  • THE SOCIAL FACTOR INVADES THE WHOLE WEB.

    Everything becomes social, even contents. Defining
    social networks becomes even more difficult.

  • PENETRATION SEEMS TO HAVE REACHED ITS LIMIT.

    The “aimless” socializing model is exhausted.

  • THE PRESENCE OF BRANDS IS NOT QUESTIONED.

    Social Networks are not exclusively for its users any-
    more: from coexistence to functionality.

  • MOBILE ACCESS TO SOCIAL NETWORKS HAS TAKEN OFF.

    The trio: networks-brands-geolocation.
